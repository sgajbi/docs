# 09 - Source Notes and Further Reading

## Purpose

This file lists useful external references for further study. The pack is a practitioner synthesis for education and platform design; it is not legal, tax, accounting, investment or regulatory advice.

## Investor and regulatory education

- Investor.gov / SEC - Exchange-Traded Funds investor bulletins and ETP education.
- Investor.gov / SEC - General investor education on investment products and market risks.
- CFTC - Learning Resources and futures market basics for futures/options and derivatives markets.
- FINRA - Exchange-traded funds and products, ETNs and investor risk alerts.
- MoneySense Singapore - Specified Investment Products and retail investor safeguards.
- MAS - Safeguards for purchasing Specified Investment Products and complex product regime materials.

## Precious metals and bullion-market references

- World Gold Council - Gold market structure, physical investment gold, gold ETFs and gold research.
- LBMA - OTC precious metals guide, precious metal accounts, Good Delivery standards and benchmark information.
- LBMA Good Delivery Lists - accredited refiners and market standards for bullion.

## Exchange and market-structure references

- CME, ICE, LME and SGX product specifications for commodity futures/options.
- Exchange settlement-price methodology and contract-expiry calendars.
- Fund/ETF/ETP prospectuses for product-specific holding, roll, fee and risk details.

## Topics for deeper future research

- Commodity index construction: price return, excess return and total return.
- Managed futures strategies and trend following.
- Commodity carry and risk premia.
- Inflation beta and regime-dependent commodity correlations.
- Gold collateral treatment and private-bank lending haircuts.
- Carbon credits and environmental markets.
- Digital gold, tokenized bullion and custody/control frameworks.
- Responsible sourcing and ESG issues in precious metals and mining.

## Product documents to collect in real projects

For each commodity product in a real platform implementation, collect:

- prospectus / termsheet / product highlights sheet;
- instrument master and classification;
- wrapper type: physical, fund, ETP, ETN, derivative, note;
- benchmark/index methodology;
- valuation source hierarchy;
- fee schedule;
- custody/provider/issuer details;
- settlement calendar and cut-off times;
- contract specifications for futures/options;
- corporate action/lifecycle event feeds;
- risk classification and suitability status;
- mandate eligibility rules;
- reporting disclosure text;
- QA test cases and sample transactions.
