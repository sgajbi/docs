## Important disclaimers for office sharing

- Insurance values are contract-specific.
- Guarantees depend on contract wording and insurer ability to pay.
- Tax and estate treatment are jurisdiction-specific.
- Product replacement, surrender, premium financing and policy loans require careful advice.
- For platform reporting, death benefit, surrender value, account value and projected value must be separately labelled.
