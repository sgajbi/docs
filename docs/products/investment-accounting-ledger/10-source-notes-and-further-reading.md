# 10 - Source Notes and Further Reading

## 1. Purpose of this source file

This pack is written as practical wealth-platform knowledge, not as a formal accounting standard interpretation. The references below are useful anchors for further study and for validating concepts such as financial-instrument classification, fair value, settlement cycle, performance-reporting principles and operational controls.

## 2. Accounting and valuation standards

| Source | Relevance |
|---|---|
| IFRS Foundation - IFRS 9 Financial Instruments | Classification and measurement, financial assets/liabilities, impairment, hedge-accounting context |
| IFRS Foundation - IFRS 13 Fair Value Measurement | Definition and framework for fair-value measurement and disclosures |
| FASB ASC Topic 820 Fair Value Measurement | U.S. GAAP fair-value framework and valuation hierarchy concepts |
| IAS 32 / IFRS 7 | Presentation and disclosure context for financial instruments |
| Local accounting policies | Bank/booking-centre implementation of accounting standards |

Important platform caveat: a wealth client-reporting platform may not be the statutory accounting ledger, but it still needs accounting-grade discipline because its outputs drive client trust, analytics and controls.

## 3. Settlement and operations references

| Source | Relevance |
