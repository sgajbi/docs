## Important regulatory nuance

The SEC adopted private fund adviser reforms in 2023, but on 5 June 2024 the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit vacated the rules. The SEC later announced that those newly adopted rules were vacated. Therefore, this knowledge pack does not treat the 2023 final rules as currently binding requirements. However, the design ideas of clear performance, fee, expense, valuation, audit and conflict reporting remain useful as strong platform-control and client-transparency principles.

## Suggested further learning topics

- ILPA reporting templates and private equity fee/expense transparency practices.
- GIPS standards for private-market performance presentation.
- IPEV valuation guidelines for private equity and venture capital valuation.
- AICPA/valuation guidance relevant to private investments.
- Fund administration capital-account statements.
- Private credit covenant and loan monitoring practices.
- Real estate appraisal standards and cap-rate valuation.
- Hedge fund risk analytics: exposure, leverage, drawdown, beta and liquidity.
