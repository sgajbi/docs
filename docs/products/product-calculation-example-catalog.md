# Product Calculation Example Catalog

This catalog provides compact calculation examples across the covered product families.

Use it when turning product knowledge into requirements, API contracts, QA scenarios, data migration checks, reporting specs, or implementation acceptance criteria. The examples are intentionally simple so the formula, source inputs, reporting labels, and failure behavior are visible.

## Calculation Pattern

Each calculation should define:

1. required source inputs,
2. formula and convention,
3. output labels,
4. reporting meaning,
5. degraded-state behavior,
6. QA assertion.

If any required input is missing, stale, estimated, disputed, restricted, or unsupported, the output should carry that state instead of looking final.

## Examples By Product Family

| Product Family | Example | Core Formula | Reporting Output | QA Assertion |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Available cash after unsettled trades | `available_cash = ledger_cash + settled_receivables - settled_payables - restrictions - reservations` | Ledger cash, settled cash, projected cash, available cash, restrictions, reservations. | A pending sell settling T+2 increases projected cash before T+2 but not available cash unless bridge funding is explicitly supported. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Fixed deposit accrued interest | `accrued_interest = principal x annual_rate x day_count_fraction` | Principal, rate, accrued interest, maturity value, maturity date, breakage terms. | Accrual uses product day-count convention and stops or changes when deposit is broken early. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Multi-currency buying power | `buying_power_ccy = purchase_ccy_available_cash + source_ccy_available_cash x fx_rate x (1 - fx_buffer)` | Purchase-currency cash, source-currency cash, FX rate, haircut/buffer, value date, linked FX requirement. | Cross-currency buying power is labelled indicative and blocked when FX execution, restrictions or settlement policy fail. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX forward mark-to-market | `mtm_reporting_ccy = present_value(forward_leg_receive - forward_leg_pay)` | Forward notional, buy/sell currency, value date, MTM, hedge link, realized P&L at settlement. | Forward notional never appears as cash balance before settlement. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cash sweep units | `estimated_units = sweep_amount / estimated_nav`; confirmed units use dealing NAV | Sweep amount, residual cash, NAV, dealing date, settlement date, reserved cash, confirmed units. | Sweep excludes restricted/reserved cash and creates units only after accepted order and confirmed NAV. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Approved overdraft funding | `usable_overdraft = approved_limit - required_buffer`; `funding_shortfall = purchase_cash_required - settled_cash` | Settled cash, projected cash, overdraft limit, utilization, buffer, facility headroom. | Purchase is blocked when the shortfall exceeds usable overdraft or mandate policy disallows leverage. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Negative interest charge | `charge = max(0, average_cash - exempt_threshold) x abs(rate) x days / day_count_basis` | Chargeable balance, rate, period, threshold, negative interest expense, booking centre. | No charge posts below threshold, and mid-period rate changes split accrual by effective date. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Gated sweep unwind liquidity | `same_day_liquidity = settled_cash + requested_redemption x gate_percentage` | Settled cash, sweep value, gate percentage, redeemable cash, pending redemption, liquidity shortfall. | Gated sweep fund value is not treated as same-day bank cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Credit-line-funded buying power | `headroom = min(facility_limit, collateral_value x (1 - haircut)) - current_exposure` | Facility limit, collateral value, haircut, exposure, headroom, reservation, funding source. | Availability fails closed when collateral price, FX rate, pledge status or facility eligibility is stale or missing. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Overdraft tiered charge | `charge = sum(tier_utilization x tier_rate x days / basis)` | Utilization by tier, rate, effective date, day count, facility status. | Financing cost accrues by tier and is reported separately from free cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday liquidity | `intraday_available = starting_cash - same_day_payments - settlement_debits - buffer` | Starting cash, payment queue, settlement windows, income timing, buffer. | Positive end-of-day projection does not override an intraday funding shortfall. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Stress liquidity | `stress_available = settled_cash + gated_redemptions + sale_proceeds_after_haircut + breakable_deposits_after_penalty` | Cash, gate percentage, sale haircut, deposit break estimate, horizon, approvals. | Stress liquidity is labelled conditional when it depends on execution, gates or approvals. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Nostro break | `cash_break = internal_ledger_cash - external_statement_cash` | Internal ledger, bank/custodian statement, value date, break components, closure state. | Unresolved positive breaks are excluded from buying power until reconciled. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cap-limited cross-bank stress liquidity | `same_day_usable = sum(min(confirmed_cash_by_bank, transfer_cap_by_bank) where cut_off_open and authority_valid)` | Bank cash, transfer cap, cut-off status, legal authority, in-transit cash, stress-usable liquidity. | Total confirmed cash is not treated as same-day usable when bank cut-offs, caps, transfer authority or confirmation status restrict movement. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Counterparty downgrade placement headroom | `revised_headroom = revised_counterparty_limit - existing_exposure`; `excess = planned_placement - revised_headroom` | Counterparty rating, rating source date, revised limit, existing exposure, placement amount, excess, approval state. | Treasury placement is blocked, resized or rerouted when downgrade reduces available counterparty headroom. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Deposit ladder repricing benefit | `net_break_benefit = principal x (new_rate - existing_rate) x days_to_maturity / basis - break_penalty` | Principal, old/new rate, maturity, penalty, quote timestamp, reinvestment tenor. | Break/reinvest recommendation is blocked or labelled indicative when rate quote or penalty source is stale. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment recall residual | `unreturned_amount = original_payment_amount - returned_amount_net_of_charges` | Original payment, returned amount, bank charges, recall state, linked FX impact. | Recall does not reverse released payment or settled FX automatically. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Booking-centre transferable cash | `transferable_cash = sum(cash where booking_centre_allowed and authority_valid and cut_off_open)` | Booking centre, legal owner, currency, documentation status, cut-off, restrictions. | Consolidated cash is not same-day transferable when booking-centre or cross-border restrictions apply. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday overdraft gap | `intraday_shortfall = same_day_debits + required_buffer - opening_settled_cash` before intraday inflows | Opening cash, debit timing, inflow timing, buffer, overdraft limit, approval state. | Positive end-of-day projection does not remove an earlier intraday funding gap. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury sweep exception amount | `exception_amount = target_sweep_amount - amount_successfully_swept` | Target sweep, swept amount, exception reason, retry date, restriction status. | Unswept cash is classified by exception reason and not treated as unexplained idle cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Trapped cash liquidity | `trapped_cash = reported_cash - same_day_usable_cash` | Bank statement cash, rail status, transfer restriction, review date, source timestamp. | Confirmed cash can be reported while excluded from available cash and settlement funding. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Blocked-market convertible value | `approved_convertible_value = approved_local_amount x fx_rate` | Onshore cash, approval amount, FX rate, approval expiry, repatriation rule. | Translated reporting value is not treated as freely transferable offshore liquidity. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Pooled cash interest allocation | `client_interest = pool_interest x client_eligible_average_balance / total_eligible_average_balance` | Pool interest, client eligible average balances, restricted balances, allocation policy, rounding. | Client allocations reconcile to pool credit and exclude non-interest-bearing balances where policy requires. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Operational cash compensation | `compensation = delayed_amount x approved_rate x delay_days / day_count_basis` | Delayed amount, incident id, approved rate, date range, basis, approval. | Compensation is reported as operational compensation, not deposit interest or market return. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment fraud recovery exposure | `unrecovered_amount = fraudulent_payment - recovered_amount` | Fraud payment, provisional credit, recovered amount, case status, final decision. | Provisional credit, recovery and final loss allocation remain separate until investigation closure. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Emergency funding shortfall | `remaining_shortfall = settlement_obligation - approved_emergency_line_available` | Settlement obligation, rail status, domestic cash, emergency line, approval, legal owner. | Reported domestic cash is excluded from same-day funding when payment rails are closed. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Haircut-adjusted usable cash | `usable_cash = sum(cash_bucket x (1 - policy_haircut))` | Ledger cash, currency, restriction state, haircut policy, booking centre, effective date. | Headline cash is separated from policy-usable cash for liquidity, mandate and pre-trade funding checks. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Liquidity transfer-pricing spread | `treasury_spread = placement_income - internal_transfer_value` | Average eligible balance, client credited rate, transfer price, placement yield, day count. | Client credited interest, internal transfer value and treasury spread are reconciled but not merged in client reporting. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Multi-entity sweepable cash | `entity_sweepable = max(0, cash - reserve) where product_eligible` | Legal entity, cash, reserve, product eligibility, authority, sweep policy. | Family-office consolidation does not imply cross-entity liquidity netting or cross-subsidy. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment-priority liquidity gap | `remaining_capacity = usable_cash - buffer - paid_priority_obligations` | Payment queue, priority, cut-off, amount, buffer, manual override. | Limited intraday liquidity is allocated by policy priority and cut-off, not arrival order alone. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Client-money segregation shortfall | `segregation_shortfall = required_client_money - external_segregated_balance` | Required client money, segregated account balance, transfer state, root cause, remediation evidence. | Segregation shortfalls are escalated, remediated and reported as control events. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cross-border payment repair rate | `repair_rate = repaired_payments / total_cross_border_payments` | Payment population, repair reason, elapsed time, release state, standing instruction state. | Repair analytics separate payment-data defects, formatting defects and screening reviews. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Money-market fund liquidity fee | `net_redemption_proceeds = gross_redemption_value - gross_redemption_value x liquidity_fee_rate` | Redeemed units, NAV, fee rate, gross redemption value, net proceeds, activation notice. | Available liquidity uses net proceeds and labels the fee separately. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Repo substitution collateral shortfall | `shortfall = required_collateral_value - replacement_market_value x (1 - haircut)` | Repo cash amount, coverage requirement, replacement collateral value, haircut, eligibility policy, settlement state. | Original collateral is not released when replacement collateral is insufficient. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday nostro forecast variance | `capacity_variance = actual_release_capacity - forecast_release_capacity` | Opening balance, expected inflows/outflows, actual inflows/outflows, buffer, cut-off, reason code. | Payment release capacity updates when expected credits miss the cut-off. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Correspondent-bank fee dispute | `excess_fee = deducted_fee - contracted_fee` | Payment principal, contracted fee, deducted fee, fee schedule, bank confirmation, dispute state. | Excess fees are tracked as disputes and reimbursed only after approved resolution. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Account-level reserve optimization | `account_investable = max(0, cash - required_reserve - upcoming_settlement)` | Account cash, reserve policy, expected fees/taxes, settlement obligations, mandate cash floor. | Consolidated investable cash reconciles to account-level reserve calculations. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX settlement netting exception | `eligible_net_amount = sum(receives) - sum(pays) for eligible trades only` | Trades, value date, counterparty, SSI state, netting set id, cut-off, exclusion reason. | Trades missing netting eligibility remain gross-settled and separately monitored. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX swap rollover failure cost | `rollover_failure_cost = replacement_usd_funding_quote - original_far_leg_usd_payable` | Original swap, far-leg payable, rejection reason, cut-off, replacement quote, settlement state. | Near-leg settlement, failed rollover exposure and replacement funding remain separately visible. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Deposit early-break net proceeds | `net_break_proceeds = principal + accrued_interest - early_break_penalty` | Principal, accrued interest, penalty, approval timestamp, quote timestamp, cut-off, dispute state. | Deposit break funding uses approved net proceeds, not normal maturity proceeds. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Virtual-account allocation break | `allocation_break = bank_credit - sum(virtual_account_allocations)` | Pooled account credit, virtual-account allocations, remittance, mapping rule, override approval. | Physical bank cash and virtual-account allocations reconcile without double counting. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment rail reroute cost | `incremental_reroute_fee = alternate_rail_fee - preferred_rail_fee` | Preferred rail, alternate rail, fees, cut-offs, outage id, revised settlement date. | Rerouted payments preserve original failure, alternate instruction, fee change and settlement impact. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury concentration-limit breach | `excess = existing_exposure + planned_placement - revised_bank_limit` | Bank exposure, revised limit, rating source, planned placement, headroom, approval state. | Treasury placement is blocked, resized, rerouted or escalated when post-placement exposure breaches limit. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Stale bank-balance certification | `certified_liquidity = 0 until balance is certified or fallback-approved` | Bank statement balance, source timestamp, certification cut-off, owner, fallback approval. | Reported bank cash is excluded from buying power when freshness or certification evidence is stale. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX overlay funding waterfall | `remaining_after_cash_and_mmf = required_usd_funding - usable_usd_cash - usable_mmf_cash` | Overlay target, funding policy, cash buckets, MMF redemption state, FX quote, credit-line terms. | Funding follows policy order and keeps FX conversion, MMF redemption and credit-line usage separately visible. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Stress-usable liquidity | `stress_usable_liquidity = confirmed_cash + gated_redemptions + breakable_deposits + unrestricted_fx_conversion` | Cash, gate percentage, deposit break quote, rail restriction, communication version. | Stress communication separates confirmed, conditional and restricted liquidity. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Negative-rate disclosure charge | `negative_interest_charge = max(0, average_cash - exempt_threshold) x abs(rate) x days / basis` | Average balance, threshold, rate, period, booking centre, disclosure version. | Client-ready reporting requires threshold, chargeable balance, rate and period disclosure. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Multi-currency pledged cash value | `eligible_collateral_value = sum(eligible_cash x fx_rate x (1 - haircut))` | Cash buckets, currencies, FX rates, haircuts, restriction state, pledge policy. | Restricted cash is excluded from collateral value unless eligibility changes with approval. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment screening ageing breach | `breach_rate = payments_over_sla / total_held_payments` | Held payments, hold timestamps, SLA, reserved cash, screening reason, owner. | Held payments reserve liquidity and escalate when ageing breaches SLA. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury exception utilization | `exception_utilization = actual_peak_exposure / temporary_approved_limit` | Normal limit, temporary limit, actual exposure, approval, expiry, attestation state. | Exception-compliant exposure is tracked separately from normal policy compliance and requires post-event attestation. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Liquidity buffer model drift | `buffer_drift = observed_required_buffer - current_model_buffer` | Settlement buffer, fee/tax reserve, withdrawal history, stress uplift, model version. | Investable cash is reduced or warning-labelled when observed liquidity need exceeds model buffer. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Beneficiary whitelist payment control | `payment_release_allowed = beneficiary_whitelisted or approved_whitelist_exception` | Payment instruction, beneficiary status, callback evidence, exception approval, cut-off. | Sufficient cash reserves liquidity but does not release payment without whitelist or approved exception. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Deposit rollover suitability conflict | `rollover_excess_over_liquidity_need = maturing_deposit - known_liquidity_need` | Maturity amount, proposed tenor, liquidity objective, rates, break penalty, approval. | Higher-rate rollover is blocked or warning-labelled when liquidity horizon conflicts with tenor. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday cash forecast confidence | `forecast_confidence_capacity = opening_certified_cash + sum(expected_inflow x confidence_score)` | Opening cash, expected inflows, confidence scores, cut-offs, source confirmations. | Payment release does not rely on raw low-confidence inflows without escalation. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Nostro overdraft fee allocation | `driver_share = driver_amount / total_overdraft_driver` | Nostro fee, intraday timeline, payment releases, settlement debits, internal delay, dispute state. | Client-chargeable and internal-caused overdraft fee portions remain separately auditable. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX conversion execution spread | `spread_bps = abs(reference_mid_rate - executed_rate) / reference_mid_rate x 10000` | Executed rate, reference mid-rate, quote timestamp, dealer/venue, tolerance, override reason. | FX conversion execution retains best-execution evidence and exceptions for stale or wide quotes. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Client cash yield tier accrual | `monthly_interest = sum(balance_period x tier_rate x days / basis)` | Cash balance periods, tier table, rate version, currency, booking centre, override evidence. | Interest accrual splits by effective-dated tier changes and preserves pricing source. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment cut-off extension release | `release_allowed = payment_time <= approved_extension_time and acknowledgement_received` | Payment instruction, standard cut-off, extension approval, correspondent acknowledgement, screening state. | One-off cut-off extensions apply only to scoped payments with approval and acknowledgement evidence. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday liquidity stress backtest | `stress_forecast_error = forecast_min_liquidity - actual_min_liquidity` | Forecast version, opening liquidity, forecast/actual payments, credits, buffer, variance threshold. | Material underestimation triggers model or policy review without automatically changing approved buffers. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cross-currency liquidity buffer | `translated_buffer = sum(currency_cash x fx_rate x (1 - haircut))` | Cash by currency, FX rate, haircut policy, restrictions, mandate floor, reporting currency. | Mandate liquidity tests use haircut-adjusted translated buffers, not nominal foreign-currency cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Returned-payment fee remediation | `client_refund_amount = return_cost if bank_or_provider_error else 0` | Return message, principal, correspondent fee, repair fee, cause classification, remediation decision. | Returned payment principal, deducted fees, remediation credit and resent instruction remain separately traceable. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX standing-instruction drift | `standing_instruction_drift = old_rule_conversion - current_policy_conversion` | Standing instruction, old threshold, current policy threshold, cash balance, consent, review date. | Standing FX conversions are blocked, warning-labelled or recertified when they conflict with current policy. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Dormant cash controlled availability | `available_cash = 0 while dormant_flag_active and reactivation_approval_pending` | Ledger cash, dormant status, last activity date, reactivation request, approval owner, payment screening state. | Ledger cash is not payment-available until reactivation and payment controls are complete. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Same-currency pooling dispute | `pooled_cash = sum(pool_account_balances)`; `disputed_fee = fee if pooling_authorization_disputed else 0` | Account balances, pooling consent, effective date, dispute evidence, fee allocation. | Standalone balances, pooled liquidity and disputed charges remain separately visible. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Negative available cash from pending fees | `available_cash = ledger_cash - sum(pending_fee_reserves)` | Ledger cash, pending custody fee, advisory fee, tax debit, waiver state, settlement state. | Positive ledger cash can still produce negative available cash when reserved obligations exceed balance. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment purpose-code release control | `payment_release_allowed = purpose_code_valid and screening_clear and funding_available` | Payment instruction, destination country, submitted purpose code, revised code, remediation state. | Cash is reserved while payment remains held for data remediation. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX holiday value-date repair | `repaired_value_date = next_common_business_day(sell_currency, buy_currency)` | Sell currency, buy currency, original value date, currency calendars, repair approval. | Calendar repair moves both FX legs consistently unless split settlement is contractually allowed. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury prefunding shortfall | `prefunding_shortfall = required_prefunding - confirmed_cash_before_cutoff` | Settlement amount, confirmed cash, expected inflow, cut-off, exception approval, attestation. | Expected unconfirmed inflows do not certify prefunding unless an exception is approved. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Account-closure residual cash | `residual_balance = late_credit_interest - late_correspondent_fee`; `closure_allowed = residual_balance == 0 or residual_disposition_instruction_confirmed` | Closure request, quote date, late interest, late fees, disposition instruction, closure approval. | Residual cash remains visible and blocks final closure until disposition is source-backed. |

| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Post-cut-off sanctions release | `same_day_release_allowed = screening_clear_time <= market_cutoff and funding_available` | Payment instruction, screening hold, release time, market cut-off, funding state, revised value date. | Payment release after cut-off rolls or exceptions settlement even when funding is available. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Deposit break quote expiry | `release_allowed = not quote_expired or renewed_quote_confirmed` | Deposit principal, accrued interest, break penalty, quote timestamp, expiry, client acceptance. | Expired break quotes require renewed source-backed confirmation before execution. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Money-market fund redemption delay | `payment_shortfall_without_redemption = linked_payment_due - confirmed_cash_before_redemption` | Redemption order, transfer-agent confirmation, expected and revised settlement dates, linked payment, confirmed cash. | Delayed redemption proceeds are pending receivable, not same-day available cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury counterparty settlement-limit excess | `limit_excess = proposed_placement - (settlement_limit - used_limit_before_placement)` | Counterparty limit, used amount, pending flows, proposed placement, exception approval, expiry. | Cash availability does not override counterparty settlement-limit controls. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Backdated cash-rate correction | `incremental_interest = eligible_average_balance x (corrected_rate - original_rate) x affected_days / day_count_basis` | Eligible average balance, original rate, corrected rate, affected days, rate table version, approval. | Post only the correction delta unless accounting policy requires reversal and repost. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday nostro cut-off shortfall | `intraday_shortfall = payment_amount - confirmed_nostro_cash_before_cutoff` | Payment amount, nostro cash, credit timestamp, cut-off, extension approval, value date. | Positive end-of-day cash does not erase an intraday release breach. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Beneficiary amendment release control | `payment_release_allowed = funding_available and screening_clear and beneficiary_whitelisted and callback_evidence_present and amendment_approved` | Original instruction, amended beneficiary, callback, whitelist state, maker/checker, cut-off. | Original beneficiary approval does not approve amended settlement details. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Liquidity forecast outage fallback | `fallback_release_capacity = last_certified_cash x (1 - fallback_haircut)` | Last certified cash, outage state, fallback haircut, payment queue, manual override, owner. | Unavailable forecast inflows are excluded from payment release capacity. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury rollover concentration excess | `concentration_excess = existing_counterparty_exposure + rollover_amount - approved_counterparty_limit` | Maturity, rollover amount, counterparty exposure, limit, tenor, exception state. | Rollover can breach concentration limits even when no new external cash is required. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX collateral substitution shortfall | `substitution_shortfall = required_collateral_value - eur_collateral x fx_rate x (1 - fx_mismatch_haircut)` | Collateral currency, FX rate, haircut, pledge agreement, substitution request, top-up state. | Existing collateral is not released until replacement value covers requirement after FX haircut. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cash statement correction reversal | `reversal_required = initial_correction_credit - bank_accepted_credit` | Original fee, correction credit, bank response, reversal, statement versions, dispute owner. | Reversal posts as linked correction with original statement and bank evidence retained. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Payment chargeback unresolved exposure | `unresolved_chargeback = provisional_credit - merchant_accepted_amount` | Client dispute, original payment, provisional credit, merchant response, scheme deadline, case state. | Provisional credit remains separate from final recovered cash until chargeback outcome is final. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury same-bank offset gap | `uncovered_deficit_after_offset = group_deficit - legally_offsettable_amount` | Entity, mandate, client-money policy, bank accounts, offset eligibility, treasury approval. | Same-bank balances are offset only within source-backed legal and operational authority. |

| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Investment committee threshold | `post_trade_weight = (existing_exposure + proposed_exposure) / consolidated_reportable_assets` | Existing exposure, proposed exposure, consolidated asset base, threshold, approval status. | Committee approval is required before order or commitment when post-trade weight breaches source-backed threshold. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | VCC sub-fund reportable exposure | `reportable_exposure = held_sub_fund_value`; non-held sub-funds are excluded unless authorized | Umbrella, sub-fund id, held value, excluded value, reporting perimeter, privacy state. | Reports do not overstate exposure by using umbrella-level assets when only one sub-fund is held. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Beneficiary-class visibility | `visible_sections = sections_allowed_by_role_and_governing_document` | Beneficiary class, permitted sections, masked sections, effective date, source document. | Named beneficiary status alone does not grant holdings, capital or trustee-note visibility. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Effective-dated authority change | `active_authority = authority where effective_from <= action_date < effective_to` | Role, authority holder, effective dates, document evidence, pending approvals. | Old authority cannot approve new actions after effective revocation or replacement date. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Letter-of-wishes entitlement control | `binding_entitlement = false unless governing_document_or_legal_review_confirms_binding_status` | Document type, version, effective date, trustee decision, disclosure policy, source evidence. | Settlor intent is preserved as context without creating automatic entitlement, authority or report access. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Charitable foundation grant utilization | `post_grant_utilization = (approved_grants + proposed_grant) / annual_grant_budget` | Grant budget, approved grants, proposed grant, council approval, recipient due diligence, purpose alignment. | Grant workflow checks budget, purpose, authority and recipient evidence before payment. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Philanthropic grant funding surplus | `funding_surplus = available_cash + eligible_settled_proceeds - proposed_grant` | Cash balance, expected proceeds, settlement state, grant amount, approval state, payment status. | Expected proceeds are not treated as usable funding until settlement policy allows. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Trust termination distributable cash | `distributable_cash = liquidation_proceeds - accrued_fees - tax_reserve` | Liquidation proceeds, fees, tax reserve, beneficiary allocation, final statement, closure state. | Final distribution and closure require zero-position, cash, reserve and obligation reconciliation. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Conflict-adjusted committee quorum | `eligible_voting_members = total_members - conflicted_members`; `quorum_met = eligible_voting_members >= quorum_requirement` | Committee membership, conflicts, recusal policy, quorum rule, votes, approval evidence. | Conflicted members are excluded when policy requires recusal, and quorum is recalculated before approval. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Protector succession active authority | `active_protector = protector where effective_from <= action_date < effective_to and acceptance_status = accepted` | Protector appointments, resignation, successor acceptance, effective dates, veto scope. | Consent-dependent actions are blocked when no accepted protector is active. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Delegated manager transition orders | `orders_requiring_reapproval = pending_orders_before_manager_change` | Manager appointment, termination reason, pending orders, due-diligence approval, effective date. | Pending orders are frozen, cancelled or reapproved when delegated authority changes. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Beneficiary loan limit | `post_loan_exposure = existing_beneficiary_loan + proposed_new_loan` | Existing loan, proposed loan, loan limit, trustee approval, interest terms, maturity. | Beneficiary loans are separate from distributions and are blocked or exceptioned when limits are breached. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Family charter threshold version | `active_rule = rule where effective_from <= proposal_date < effective_to` | Charter version, approval threshold, proposal date, approval date, amendment evidence. | Governance thresholds apply by effective-dated rule rather than informal intent. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Multi-branch reportable value | `authorized_reportable_value = sum(values where recipient_entitled)` | Booking centre, recipient role, restricted assets, external custodian values, masking rule. | Branch-specific reports exclude or mask assets outside recipient authority. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Charitable pledge cancellation | `remaining_pledge = total_approved_pledge - paid_installments` | Pledge approval, paid installments, due-diligence result, cancellation decision, reserved liquidity. | Future installments stop while completed grants remain preserved with evidence. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Excluded-person access control | `report_access_allowed = recipient_not_excluded and recipient_role_entitled` | Exclusion clause, named person, relationship, effective dates, request type, legal review evidence. | Excluded persons are blocked from distributions, loans, authority and report access without exposing restricted details. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Foundation redomiciliation active domicile | `active_domicile = domicile where effective_from <= action_date < effective_to` | Prior domicile, new domicile, registry evidence, legal opinion, effective date, documentation tasks. | Reporting and documentation rules change from the effective date with historical domicile preserved. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Emergency trustee authority | `emergency_action_allowed = emergency_clause_active and action_purpose == asset_preservation` | Emergency clause, triggering event, action purpose, trustee decision, market cut-off, ratification deadline. | Emergency powers allow only scoped protective actions and require post-event ratification. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Private-trust-company board revalidation | `approvals_requiring_revalidation = pending_approvals_at_board_change` | Director appointments, resignations, acceptance, register update, quorum rule, pending approvals. | Board changes recalculate quorum, signing authority and pending approval validity. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Beneficiary consent campaign coverage | `consent_coverage = received_consents / required_consents` | Beneficiary class, notice package, consent right, response state, representative authority, deadline. | Final restructuring is blocked until required consent threshold and trustee approval are met. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Family-office expense dispute | `disputed_expense = proposed_allocation - accepted_allocation` | Expense policy, service usage basis, allocation run, disputed amount, approval owner, resolution state. | Accepted, disputed and reallocated expenses are reported separately by recipient authority. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Reserved powers after incapacity | `settlor_investment_authority_active = reserved_power_active and incapacity_status != confirmed` | Reserved-power clause, capacity evidence, trustee fallback authority, pending instructions, effective date. | Confirmed incapacity suspends reserved investment authority where the governing document requires it. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Letter-of-wishes disclosure control | `disclosure_allowed = trustee_decision == approved and recipient_scope_allows_document` | Disclosure request, objection, trustee decision, document sections, confidentiality scope, legal review. | Letter-of-wishes visibility follows trustee-approved disclosure scope, not beneficiary status alone. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Trust-to-company transfer continuity | `consolidated_family_group_change = group_value_after_transfer - group_value_before_transfer` | Transfer deed, trustee approval, company approval, valuation basis, beneficial-owner linkage, pledge status. | Entity-level holdings move while consolidated family reporting avoids sale treatment and double counting. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Protector recusal route | `alternate_route_required = consent_required and conflict_declared == true` | Protector consent right, conflict declaration, affected transaction, alternate authority, consent evidence. | Conflicted protector approval is blocked for scoped transactions and routed to permitted alternate authority. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Service-level chargeback rate | `chargeback_rate = service_charge / charge_base` | Service tier, charge base, SLA evidence, allocation run, approval, dispute state. | Premium service costs are allocated only to entities that received the service. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Estate reserve release | `approved_release = remaining_reserve - retained_contingency` | Reserve policy, settled obligations, retained contingency, administrator approval, beneficiary allocation. | Estate reserve releases separate settled obligations, retained reserve and beneficiary cash release. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Purpose trust budget headroom | `available_budget_headroom = approved_budget - committed_spend - required_contingency` | Trust deed, approved budget, enforcer review, trustee approval, invoice, purpose evidence. | Operating payments that exceed approved headroom require supplemental approval and are not beneficiary distributions. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Privacy masking appeal outcome | `denied_sections = requested_sections - approved_sections` | Appeal request, beneficiary class, trustee decision, masking rule version, approved sections, denied sections. | Visibility expands only for trustee-approved sections and effective dates. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Cross-border probate interim distribution | `interim_distributable = domestic_liquid_assets - known_liabilities - retained_contingency` | Death certificate, domestic grant, foreign probate status, executor approval, liability schedule. | Foreign probate-restricted assets remain excluded from final distribution and estate closure. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Governance voting deadlock | `decision_deadlocked = quorum_met and majority_met == false and votes_for == votes_against` | Charter version, attendance, votes, conflicts, quorum rule, escalation mechanism. | Quorum alone does not authorize implementation when voting threshold fails. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Trustee fee tier dispute | `disputed_fee = charged_fee - contracted_tiered_fee` | Trustee invoice, fee schedule, asset base, calculation version, dispute notice, allocation policy. | Charged, contracted, accepted and disputed fees remain separate before beneficiary allocation. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Foundation dissolution distributable amount | `final_distributable = foundation_assets - known_liabilities - restricted_reserve - wind_down_costs` | Council approval, registry dissolution evidence, successor recipient due diligence, liability schedule, reserve basis. | Distribution occurs only after liabilities, reserves, wind-down costs and recipient eligibility are evidenced. |
