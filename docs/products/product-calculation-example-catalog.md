# Product Calculation Example Catalog

This catalog provides compact calculation examples across the covered product families.

Use it when turning product knowledge into requirements, API contracts, QA scenarios, data migration checks, reporting specs, or implementation acceptance criteria. The examples are intentionally simple so the formula, source inputs, reporting labels, and failure behavior are visible.

For lifecycle-heavy examples, also use [`cross-product-worked-examples.md`](cross-product-worked-examples.md). For deeper calculation case studies and golden QA scenarios, use [`cross-product-worked-examples-calculations-case-studies/`](cross-product-worked-examples-calculations-case-studies/README.md). For performance, attribution, and risk treatment, use [`product-performance-attribution-risk-guide.md`](product-performance-attribution-risk-guide.md). For source ownership and failure states, use [`source-ownership-calculation-reporting-matrix.md`](source-ownership-calculation-reporting-matrix.md). ## Calculation Pattern

Each calculation should define:

1. required source inputs,
2. formula and convention,
3. output labels,
4. reporting meaning,
5. degraded-state behavior,
6. QA assertion.

If any required input is missing, stale, estimated, disputed, restricted, or unsupported, the output should carry that state instead of looking final.

## Examples By Product Family

| Product Family | Example | Core Formula | Reporting Output | QA Assertion |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Available cash after unsettled trades | `available_cash = ledger_cash + settled_receivables - settled_payables - restrictions - reservations` | Ledger cash, settled cash, projected cash, available cash, restrictions, reservations. | A pending sell settling T+2 increases projected cash before T+2 but not available cash unless bridge funding is explicitly supported. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Fixed deposit accrued interest | `accrued_interest = principal x annual_rate x day_count_fraction` | Principal, rate, accrued interest, maturity value, maturity date, breakage terms. | Accrual uses product day-count convention and stops or changes when deposit is broken early. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Multi-currency buying power | `buying_power_ccy = purchase_ccy_available_cash + source_ccy_available_cash x fx_rate x (1 - fx_buffer)` | Purchase-currency cash, source-currency cash, FX rate, haircut/buffer, value date, linked FX requirement. | Cross-currency buying power is labelled indicative and blocked when FX execution, restrictions or settlement policy fail. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | FX forward mark-to-market | `mtm_reporting_ccy = present_value(forward_leg_receive - forward_leg_pay)` | Forward notional, buy/sell currency, value date, MTM, hedge link, realized P&L at settlement. | Forward notional never appears as cash balance before settlement. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cash sweep units | `estimated_units = sweep_amount / estimated_nav`; confirmed units use dealing NAV | Sweep amount, residual cash, NAV, dealing date, settlement date, reserved cash, confirmed units. | Sweep excludes restricted/reserved cash and creates units only after accepted order and confirmed NAV. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Approved overdraft funding | `usable_overdraft = approved_limit - required_buffer`; `funding_shortfall = purchase_cash_required - settled_cash` | Settled cash, projected cash, overdraft limit, utilization, buffer, facility headroom. | Purchase is blocked when the shortfall exceeds usable overdraft or mandate policy disallows leverage. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Negative interest charge | `charge = max(0, average_cash - exempt_threshold) x abs(rate) x days / day_count_basis` | Chargeable balance, rate, period, threshold, negative interest expense, booking centre. | No charge posts below threshold, and mid-period rate changes split accrual by effective date. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Gated sweep unwind liquidity | `same_day_liquidity = settled_cash + requested_redemption x gate_percentage` | Settled cash, sweep value, gate percentage, redeemable cash, pending redemption, liquidity shortfall. | Gated sweep fund value is not treated as same-day bank cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Credit-line-funded buying power | `headroom = min(facility_limit, collateral_value x (1 - haircut)) - current_exposure` | Facility limit, collateral value, haircut, exposure, headroom, reservation, funding source. | Availability fails closed when collateral price, FX rate, pledge status or facility eligibility is stale or missing. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Overdraft tiered charge | `charge = sum(tier_utilization x tier_rate x days / basis)` | Utilization by tier, rate, effective date, day count, facility status. | Financing cost accrues by tier and is reported separately from free cash. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Intraday liquidity | `intraday_available = starting_cash - same_day_payments - settlement_debits - buffer` | Starting cash, payment queue, settlement windows, income timing, buffer. | Positive end-of-day projection does not override an intraday funding shortfall. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Stress liquidity | `stress_available = settled_cash + gated_redemptions + sale_proceeds_after_haircut + breakable_deposits_after_penalty` | Cash, gate percentage, sale haircut, deposit break estimate, horizon, approvals. | Stress liquidity is labelled conditional when it depends on execution, gates or approvals. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Nostro break | `cash_break = internal_ledger_cash - external_statement_cash` | Internal ledger, bank/custodian statement, value date, break components, closure state. | Unresolved positive breaks are excluded from buying power until reconciled. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Cap-limited cross-bank stress liquidity | `same_day_usable = sum(min(confirmed_cash_by_bank, transfer_cap_by_bank) where cut_off_open and authority_valid)` | Bank cash, transfer cap, cut-off status, legal authority, in-transit cash, stress-usable liquidity. | Total confirmed cash is not treated as same-day usable when bank cut-offs, caps, transfer authority or confirmation status restrict movement. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Counterparty downgrade placement headroom | `revised_headroom = revised_counterparty_limit - existing_exposure`; `excess = planned_placement - revised_headroom` | Counterparty rating, rating source date, revised limit, existing exposure, placement amount, excess, approval state. | Treasury placement is blocked, resized or rerouted when downgrade reduces available counterparty headroom. |
| Client, account, portfolio and relationship master data | Joint-account approval threshold | `approval_required = action in restricted_actions or product_complexity >= threshold` | Joint owners, action type, product complexity, required approvers, approval state. | Complex-product actions require configured joint approval even when one owner can view the account. |
| Client, account, portfolio and relationship master data | Account transfer reporting split | `report_scope = pre_transfer_activity(old_account) + post_transfer_activity(new_account)` | Old account, new account, transfer date, activity split, tax-lot migration state. | Historical reports do not overwrite old account identity with new booking-centre identifier. |
| Client, account, portfolio and relationship master data | Effective-dated beneficial owner | `active_beneficial_owner = owner where effective_from <= report_date < effective_to` | Beneficial owner, legal owner, effective dates, documentation status, remediation state. | Beneficial-owner changes do not alter legal owner or historical reporting basis unless source-backed. |
| Client, account, portfolio and relationship master data | Advisor coverage change | `active_advisor = advisor where effective_from <= action_date < effective_to` | Advisor, coverage team, effective date, report delivery list, task ownership. | Historical actions retain prior advisor while current entitlements and tasks move to new coverage. |
| Bonds | Dirty market value | `dirty_value = nominal x dirty_price / price_factor` | Clean value, accrued interest, dirty value, local/reporting currency value. | Clean value plus accrued interest equals dirty value within rounding tolerance. |
| Bonds | Coupon accrual | `accrued_interest = nominal x coupon_rate x day_count_fraction` | Accrued income, coupon receivable, income report, accrued-interest source date. | Ex-coupon or default state changes accrual treatment according to source terms. |
| Bonds | Yield-to-worst | `yield_to_worst = min(yield_to_each_valid_call_or_maturity_date)` | Yield-to-maturity, yield-to-call, yield-to-worst, call schedule, maturity ladder. | Yield-to-worst is blocked when callable schedule or price basis is missing. |
| Bonds | Inflation-linked adjusted principal | `adjusted_principal = original_nominal x current_index / base_index` | Index ratio, adjusted principal, real coupon basis, clean market value, index source date. | Stale CPI or missing index source blocks final adjusted-principal reporting or labels it stale. |
| Bonds | Amortising outstanding principal | `ending_principal = opening_principal - scheduled_principal - unscheduled_principal` | Opening principal, coupon basis, principal repayment, closing principal, maturity ladder. | Coupon accrual after repayment uses reduced principal from the effective schedule date. |
| Bonds | Convertible conversion value | `conversion_value = nominal / conversion_price x underlying_price` | Shares on conversion, conversion value, bond floor, analytical equity exposure. | Direct equity position is created only after confirmed conversion, not while optionality is analytical. |
| Bonds | ABS factor and prepayment | `ending_factor = ending_current_principal / original_principal` | Current principal, scheduled principal, unscheduled prepayment, factor, remittance date. | Prepayment is classified as principal return and updates yield/WAL assumptions with source evidence. |
| Bonds | Multi-currency performance | `base_return = (end_local_value x end_fx - start_local_value x start_fx) / (start_local_value x start_fx)` | Local return, reporting-currency return, FX effect, income conversion, hedge effect. | Local security return and FX translation effect are explainable separately. |
| Bonds | Sinkable principal reduction | `remaining_nominal = opening_nominal - redeemed_nominal`; `future_coupon = remaining_nominal x coupon_rate` | Opening nominal, sink amount, remaining nominal, redemption cash, future coupon basis. | Scheduled sink events reduce current face and future accruals instead of being treated as ordinary income. |
| Bonds | Make-whole call settlement | `total_redemption_cash = nominal x call_price / 100 + nominal x accrued_interest_per_100 / 100` | Call price, accrued interest, principal proceeds, income settlement, event source. | Make-whole premium is separated from accrued interest and is not defaulted to par when source price is missing. |
| Bonds | Distressed exchange package value | `package_value = new_note_nominal x new_note_price / 100 + cash_consideration` | Old nominal, exchange ratio, new note nominal, cash consideration, package value, event lineage. | New securities are created only after source-confirmed exchange terms and identifiers. |
| Bonds | Bond tax-lot realized result | `realized_result = net_sale_proceeds - allocated_lot_cost` | Sold nominal, lot allocation, clean proceeds, commission, realized result, remaining lots. | Lot selection follows configured policy and accrued interest settlement remains separate from clean-price result. |
| Bonds | Accrued-interest correction | `correction = corrected_accrued_interest - original_accrued_interest` | Original accrued interest, corrected accrued interest, cash/accrual delta, reason code. | Corrections preserve original values and restate income/cash with lineage rather than appearing as market price movement. |
| Bonds | Cross-currency hedged result | `hedged_result = unhedged_reporting_ccy_result + hedge_pnl` | Local return, FX effect, hedge P&L, residual exposure, hedge link. | Hedge attribution is shown only when the hedge link is source-backed; otherwise derivative P&L remains separate. |
| Bonds | Tender offer proceeds | `cash_received = accepted_nominal x tender_price / 100 + accepted_nominal x accrued_interest_per_100 / 100` | Tendered nominal, accepted nominal, tender price, accrued interest, remaining nominal. | Position reduces only by accepted nominal and accrued interest remains separate from tender premium. |
| Bonds | Consent fee | `consent_fee_cash = eligible_nominal x fee_per_100 / 100` | Eligible nominal, consent status, fee rate, cash fee, covenant amendment status. | Fee posts only after source-accepted consent and amendment terms are tracked separately. |
| Bonds | Contingent convertible write-down | `remaining_nominal = opening_nominal x (1 - write_down_percentage)` | Trigger notice, opening nominal, write-down percentage, remaining nominal, coupon state. | Trigger processing requires confirmed source notice; rumors do not create accounting postings. |
| Bonds | Perpetual reset coupon | `reset_coupon = benchmark_rate + reset_spread` | Benchmark fixing, reset spread, reset date, coupon rate, projected coupon. | First call date is not treated as legal maturity and reset accrual waits for source fixing. |
| Bonds | Multi-book bond basis | `basis_difference = fair_value - amortized_cost` | Accounting book, fair-value book, tax book, accrued interest, effective date. | Reports use the requested book basis and do not silently substitute another valuation basis. |
| Bonds | Repo-funded bond equity and financing cost | `repo_cash = dirty_value x (1 - haircut)`; `equity_funding = dirty_value - repo_cash`; `repo_interest = repo_cash x repo_rate x days / basis` | Bond value, repo liability, client equity funding, repo rate, financing cost, roll date. | Repo cash is financing, not investment income, and reports show bond exposure separately from repo liability. |
| Bonds | Duration-hedge roll sizing | `next_contracts = target_hedge_dv01 / next_contract_dv01` | Current hedge, next contract, target DV01, residual DV01, roll date, overlap state. | Roll workflow prevents unmeasured hedge gaps or double exposure when old and new futures overlap. |
| Equities | Dividend entitlement | `gross_dividend = eligible_shares x dividend_per_share` | Gross dividend, tax withheld, net cash, ex-date, record date, pay date. | Shares bought after ex-date do not receive dividend even if held on pay date. |
| Equities | Realized P&L on partial sale | `realized_pnl = sale_proceeds - allocated_cost_basis - fees - tax` | Proceeds, cost basis, fees, tax, realized gain/loss, remaining tax lots. | FIFO/average/specific-lot method is explicit and repeatable. |
| Equities | Stock split quantity | `new_quantity = old_quantity x split_ratio` | Adjusted quantity, adjusted cost per share, unchanged total cost basis. | Total cost basis remains unchanged after split unless cash-in-lieu applies. |
| Funds | NAV-based market value | `market_value = units x nav_per_unit` | Units, NAV, NAV date, market value, stale NAV flag. | Market value is stale when NAV date breaches policy threshold. |
| Funds | Redemption settlement | `cash_settlement = redeemed_units x dealing_nav - fees - tax` | Redeemed units, remaining units, settlement cash, dealing NAV, order status. | Gated redemption books only confirmed portion and leaves residual pending or invested. |
| Funds | Distribution reinvestment | `new_units = net_distribution / reinvestment_nav` | Gross distribution, tax, net reinvestment, new units, NAV. | Reinvested distribution increases units, not cash, unless cash payout elected. |
| Funds | Equalization credit | `equalization_credit = confirmed_units x equalization_credit_per_unit` | Confirmed units, dealing NAV, equalization credit, series/equalization method, administrator source date. | Equalization is reported as fee-allocation economics and not booked as cash unless administrator confirms cash movement. |
| Funds | Share-class conversion | `new_units = (old_units x old_nav - conversion_fee) / new_class_nav` | Old class, new class, conversion fee, new units, cost/tax policy, event lineage. | Conversion does not look like ordinary sale and purchase unless policy explicitly treats it as disposal. |
| Funds | Fund merger exchange | `new_units = old_units x exchange_ratio`; cash in lieu is separate | Old fund, receiving fund, exchange ratio, new units, cash in lieu, effective date. | New position is created only after receiving fund and exchange ratio are source-confirmed. |
| Funds | Redemption holdback | `holdback_receivable = redemption_value x holdback_percentage` | Initial payout, holdback receivable, notice date, settlement date, final adjustment. | Holdback receivable is not available cash until final administrator confirmation. |
| Funds | Fund-of-funds look-through coverage | `total_coverage = sum(layer_weight x layer_coverage)` | Effective coverage, mapped exposure, unmapped exposure, source date by layer. | Partial look-through reports unmapped residual exposure instead of presenting full coverage. |
| Funds | Equalization after partial redemption | `net_redemption_value = redeemed_units x nav - equalization_debit` | Redeemed units, NAV, equalization debit/credit, net proceeds, remaining units. | Equalization follows administrator series or lot data and is not averaged across unrelated holdings. |
| Funds | Performance-fee crystallization | `performance_fee_per_unit = max(pre_fee_nav - high_water_mark - hurdle, 0) x fee_rate` | High-water mark, hurdle, fee rate, pre-fee NAV, post-fee NAV, fee basis. | Embedded performance fees are not double-booked as separate cash fees unless administrator confirms cash movement. |
| Funds | In-specie redemption settlement | `total_settlement_value = cash_component + sum(delivered_asset_values)` | Fund units redeemed, cash component, delivered securities, settlement value, reconciliation state. | Delivered securities become holdings only after custodian confirmation and instrument setup. |
| Funds | Custody re-registration | `target_units_after = target_units_before + transferred_units`; source decreases by the same units | Source custodian, target custodian, transferred units, owner, transfer value reference. | Re-registration does not create sale, redemption, subscription, realized gain or client cashflow when beneficial ownership is unchanged. |
| Funds | Stale look-through override | `override_active = report_date <= override_expiry_date` | Holdings date, freshness threshold, override approver, expiry date, degraded state. | Look-through analytics degrade or block after override expiry unless a new source file or approval is present. |
| Funds | Trailer-fee rebate | `rebate = average_eligible_value x trailer_rate x days / basis` | Eligible value, service model, share class, rebate rate, period, cash/receivable state. | Rebate is classified as fee rebate and blocked when clean-fee class already removes trailer economics. |
| Funds | Anti-dilution levy correction | `cash_correction = original_levy - corrected_levy` | Original levy, corrected levy, redemption value, dispute reason, source notice. | Levy correction preserves original redemption and posts linked receivable/cash adjustment. |
| Funds | Side-pocket realization | `cash_proceeds = side_pocket_units x realized_proceeds_per_unit` | Side-pocket units, last NAV, realized proceeds, residual units, administrator notice. | Realized proceeds do not imply full side-pocket exit unless residual position is confirmed closed. |
| Funds | Master-feeder restructure | `new_feeder_units = old_feeder_units x exchange_ratio` | Old feeder, new feeder, exchange ratio, fee terms, liquidity terms, master exposure. | New feeder position is created only from source-confirmed restructure terms and avoids look-through double counting. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Precious-metal market value | `market_value = quantity x price_per_unit x fx_rate` | Quantity, unit, price source, market value, FX, custody/provider. | Troy ounce, gram, kilogram, or contract unit must match price unit before publishing value. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Futures notional exposure | `notional = futures_price x contract_multiplier x contract_count` | Contract count, multiplier, notional exposure, margin cash, variation margin. | Margin cash is not substituted for notional exposure in risk reporting. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Pledged metal lending value | `lending_value = pledged_quantity x price_per_unit x fx_rate x (1 - haircut)` | Pledged quantity, market value, haircut, lending value, availability. | Unpledged quantity is excluded from lending value even if held in the account. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Storage-fee accrual | `storage_fee = metal_value x annual_fee_rate x days / day_count_basis` | Metal value, fee rate, accrual period, expense, invoice reconciliation state. | Storage fees are expense items and do not reduce reported commodity price return unless performance methodology explicitly nets them. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Paid-in-kind metal fee | `ending_quantity = opening_quantity - fee_quantity`; `fee_value = fee_quantity x price_per_unit` | Opening quantity, fee quantity, ending quantity, fee value, provider statement. | Quantity reduction and expense are both visible, and the event is not misclassified as an ordinary client sale without policy support. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Commodity option intrinsic value | `intrinsic_value = max(futures_price - strike, 0) x multiplier x contracts` for a call | Option terms, strike, futures price, multiplier, intrinsic value, settlement method. | Exercise creates cash settlement or a resulting futures position according to contract terms, not an unsupported physical commodity holding. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Warehouse receipt value | `receipt_value = quantity x grade_location_adjusted_price` | Receipt id, quantity, grade, location, provider, encumbrance state, value. | Missing receipt identity, lien status, grade or location blocks client-ready reporting or marks the value as support-limited. |
| Commodities, precious metals and real assets | Commodity index roll impact | `roll_impact = (new_contract_price - old_contract_price) x multiplier x contracts` with sign by position direction | Old contract, new contract, curve state, roll cost/benefit, spot return, collateral return. | Roll impact is separated from spot return, collateral return, fees and FX. |
| Insurance and annuities | Net surrender value | `net_surrender_value = account_or_cash_value - surrender_charges - policy_loans - accrued_loan_interest` | Account value, cash value, surrender value, loan balance, quote date. | Death benefit and projected value are never used as accessible surrender cash. |
| Insurance and annuities | Premium sufficiency | `coverage_status = active if paid_to_date >= required_paid_to_date else grace_or_lapse_workflow` | Premium due, paid-to date, grace period, lapse risk, policy status. | Missed premium creates a visible lifecycle state and does not silently keep policy fully active. |
| Insurance and annuities | Annuity annual income | `annual_income = periodic_payment x payments_per_year` | Payment amount, frequency, start date, end/lifetime basis, guarantee period. | Annuitized income is reported as income stream, not liquid market value unless source provides surrender value. |
| Loans, Lombard, margin and collateral | Lombard availability | `availability = min(facility_limit - exposure, collateral_lending_value - exposure)` | Facility limit, exposure, collateral value, lending value, headroom, availability. | Availability fails closed when collateral price, FX, haircut, or pledge state is stale or missing. |
| Loans, Lombard, margin and collateral | Margin shortfall | `shortfall = max(0, exposure - required_collateral_value_after_haircut)` | Exposure, required collateral, actual lending value, shortfall, cure deadline. | Price shock that breaches threshold creates margin-call workflow with cure options. |
| Loans, Lombard, margin and collateral | Loan interest accrual | `accrued_interest = drawn_principal x rate x day_count_fraction` | Drawn principal, rate, accrued interest, next payment date, utilization. | Interest accrual follows loan day-count convention and rate reset schedule. |
| Private markets | Unfunded commitment | `unfunded = commitment - paid_in + recallable_capital - released_commitment` | Commitment, paid-in, unfunded, recallable, remaining obligation. | Negative unfunded requires exception review unless source explicitly supports overcall/adjustment. |
| Private markets | TVPI, DPI, RVPI | `TVPI = (distributions + residual_value) / paid_in`; `DPI = distributions / paid_in`; `RVPI = residual_value / paid_in` | Paid-in, distributions, NAV, TVPI, DPI, RVPI, valuation date. | Multiples are marked partial when historical cashflows or terminal NAV are incomplete. |
| Private markets | IRR | `IRR = discount_rate where NPV(cashflows + terminal_value) = 0` | IRR, cashflow history, terminal NAV, valuation date, partial-history flag. | IRR is unavailable when dated cashflow history is incomplete. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | REIT distribution yield | `distribution_yield = annualized_distribution_per_unit / market_price` | Distribution yield, distribution history, market price, income caveat. | Distribution yield is labelled historical/indicated and not guaranteed. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Real-asset look-through allocation | `sector_exposure = market_value x source_weight` | Sector/geography exposure, source date, coverage percentage. | Look-through exposure is partial when source coverage is below policy threshold. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Appraisal-based value | `reported_value = appraised_value x ownership_percentage x fx_rate` | Appraised value, ownership percentage, valuation date, received date, stale state. | Appraisal value is not reported as current market-traded value. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Tenant concentration | `top_tenant_concentration = largest_tenant_rent / total_annual_rent` | Tenant concentration, top tenant share, top tenant group share, source date, confidentiality label. | Tenant analytics are partial or masked when source coverage or confidentiality restrictions apply. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Occupancy income impact | `estimated_rent_change = potential_gross_rent x (new_occupancy - prior_occupancy)` | Prior occupancy, current occupancy, potential rent, estimated income impact, source date. | Occupancy change updates analytics and projections but does not book cash by itself. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Weighted average lease expiry | `WALE = sum(annual_rent x years_to_expiry) / total_annual_rent` | WALE, lease expiry buckets, rent basis, source date, partial coverage. | WALE label states whether it is rent-weighted, area-weighted or another source basis. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Direct-property ownership value | `reportable_value = appraised_value x ownership_percentage` | Ownership percentage, reportable value, appraisal date, legal owner, beneficial owner. | Ownership transfer updates hierarchy and values without duplicating full property value across owners. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Development cost-to-complete | `uncommitted_budget_headroom = total_budget - incurred_cost - committed_unpaid_cost - contingency_reserve` | Budget, incurred cost, committed cost, contingency, headroom, milestone state. | Development exposure is labelled separately from stabilized property value and income. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Construction drawdown | `cash_required = commitment x drawdown_percentage`; `unfunded_after = commitment - paid_in_after_drawdown` | Commitment, paid-in, drawdown percentage, cash due, unfunded commitment, due date. | Drawdowns create liquidity obligations and project lineage before NAV is updated. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Effective rent after rent-free period | `effective_annual_rent = (headline_rent x lease_years - rent_free_value) / lease_years` | Headline rent, rent-free period, effective rent, cash-rent start date. | Headline rent is not reported as immediate recurring cash income while rent-free terms apply. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Property sale net proceeds | `net_cash_after_loan_payoff = sale_price - selling_costs - loan_payoff` | Sale price, costs, debt payoff, net cash, completion state, collateral release. | Property exposure closes only after completion evidence, not merely signed sale agreement. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Valuation committee override | `override_value = appraisal_value x (1 - override_haircut)` | Appraisal value, adjusted value, approver, reason, effective date, expiry. | Committee-adjusted value preserves original appraisal and expires without renewed evidence. |
| Real estate, REITs and infrastructure | Infrastructure revenue clawback | `clawback_obligation = max(actual_revenue - allowed_revenue, 0) x clawback_percentage` | Allowed revenue, actual revenue, excess, clawback, dispute state, future tariff impact. | Clawback is separated from sustainable recurring income and future revenue projections. |
| Derivatives | Option premium cashflow | `premium_cash = option_price x contract_multiplier x contract_count` | Premium paid/received, contract count, multiplier, fees, cash movement. | Premium cashflow is separate from later MTM changes. |
| Derivatives | Delta-adjusted exposure | `delta_exposure = underlying_price x multiplier x contracts x delta` | Notional exposure, delta, delta-adjusted exposure, underlying, valuation date. | Exposure is unavailable when delta or multiplier is missing. |
| Derivatives | Futures daily variation margin | `variation_margin = (settlement_price_today - settlement_price_prior) x multiplier x contracts` | Daily cash movement, realized daily P&L, notional exposure, margin balance. | Variation margin changes cash but does not close the futures contract. |
| Derivatives | Bull call spread payoff | `net_pnl = min(max(underlying - long_strike, 0), short_strike - long_strike) x multiplier x contracts - net_premium` | Leg-level positions, strategy payoff, maximum loss, maximum gain, breakeven. | Missing or stale leg data blocks strategy-level payoff while preserving legal leg holdings. |
| Derivatives | Hedge effectiveness offset | `offset_ratio = hedge_pnl / hedged_exposure_loss` with explicit sign convention | Hedged exposure, hedge notional, hedge P&L, residual exposure, effectiveness basis. | Hedge report separates underlying asset return, currency/market effect, hedge P&L and residual exposure. |
| Derivatives | Collateral surplus under CSA | `surplus = sum(collateral_market_value x (1 - haircut)) - collateral_requirement` | Eligible collateral, haircut, lending value, requirement, surplus/shortfall, substitution candidates. | Stale collateral value, ineligible asset or missing CSA term prevents optimized pledge recommendation. |
| Derivatives | Portfolio Greeks aggregation | `aggregate_delta = sum(contracts x multiplier x delta)` and equivalent aggregation by risk factor for gamma/vega | Position Greeks, source date, aggregate delta/gamma/vega, partial coverage flag. | Aggregation is grouped by compatible underlying/risk factor and labels stale or missing sensitivities. |
| Derivatives | Variance swap settlement | `settlement = (realized_volatility^2 - strike_volatility^2) x variance_notional` | Realized volatility, strike volatility, variance notional, observation period, cap/floor terms. | Settlement uses realized variance methodology and source observations, not simple underlying return. |
| Derivatives | Barrier option state | `barrier_breached = observed_level >= barrier_level` for an up barrier, adjusted for observation rule | Barrier level, observed level, observation rule, breach date, rebate, lifecycle state. | Barrier events are processed only from source-backed observation terms and price evidence. |
| Derivatives | Cross-currency swap cashflow split | `net_cashflow_by_date = coupon_leg_a + coupon_leg_b + notional_exchange_if_due` | Currency notionals, coupon legs, exchange dates, FX rates, valuation source. | Initial/final notional exchanges are separated from periodic coupon cashflows and MTM valuation. |
| Derivatives | Collateral dispute amount | `dispute_amount = counterparty_call - internal_supportable_call` | Counterparty call, internal call, threshold, agreed amount, disputed amount, due date. | Disputed collateral is tracked separately from posted/agreed collateral and free liquidity. |
| Derivatives | Clearing-broker default exposure | `affected_margin = initial_margin + variation_margin + excess_collateral` | Clearing broker, affected contracts, margin, porting state, close-out state. | Ported contracts update broker metadata with lineage; failed porting triggers close-out workflow. |
| Derivatives | Strategy-level option attribution | `strategy_contribution = sum(leg_pnl) - premium_amortization - fees` | Leg P&L, group id, valuation dates, premium/fees, strategy contribution. | Strategy attribution is partial or blocked when any legal leg or current valuation is missing. |
| Derivatives | SIMM-style margin input completeness | `complete = all(required_sensitivity_files + netting_set + threshold + valuation_date present)` | Risk sensitivities, product class, bucket, currency, netting set, threshold, independent amount, model version. | Margin estimate is partial or blocked when mandatory source inputs are stale, missing or not legally nettable. |
| Derivatives | Volatility-surface shock | `estimated_value_change = aggregate_vega x volatility_shock` | Vega, underlying, expiry bucket, volatility surface source, shock size, valuation date. | Vega-based shock is labelled approximate unless a full model revaluation is source-backed. |
| Derivatives | Callable swap exercise value | `net_exercise_value = current_mtm - break_funding_cost - fees` | Call date, notice deadline, dealer valuation, break funding, fees, approval state. | Exercise workflow is blocked when notice deadline, dealer valuation or authority evidence is missing. |
| Derivatives | Equity swap dividend adjustment | `dividend_adjustment = actual_dividend - expected_dividend` | Basket composition, expected dividend, actual dividend, source date, dealer statement, financing leg. | Synthetic dividend return is not reported as physical share income. |
| Derivatives | Futures delivery-risk control | `delivery_risk = open_contracts > 0 and today >= first_notice_alert_date` | Contract, open quantity, first notice day, last trade date, delivery window, roll/close instruction. | Physical delivery workflow starts only from source delivery notice evidence. |
| Derivatives | Give-up acceptance state | `clearing_ready = execution_confirmed and give_up_accepted and account_mapping_valid` | Executing broker, clearing broker, account, give-up id, acceptance/rejection state, repair lineage. | Rejected give-ups remain pending/exceptioned and cannot duplicate settled positions. |
| Derivatives | Early termination cash | `termination_cash = close_out_mtm + accrued_receivable - break_funding_charge - fees` | Close-out MTM, accruals, break funding, fees, full/partial termination state, collateral release. | Termination does not close the contract or release collateral without source confirmation. |
| Derivatives | Close-out netting exposure | `collateral_adjusted_exposure = sum(nettable_trade_mtm) - posted_collateral` | Netting set, trade MTMs, legal agreement, posted collateral, independent amount, dispute state. | Only enforceable netting-set trades are netted and independent amount disputes are shown separately. |
| Structured products | Conditional coupon | `coupon = nominal x coupon_rate x accrual_fraction if observation_condition_met else 0` | Coupon status, observation date, barrier/coupon condition, payable amount. | Coupon remains pending until observation result and issuer notice are confirmed. |
| Structured products | Barrier distance | `barrier_distance = (underlying_level - barrier_level) / underlying_level` | Barrier level, underlying level, distance, observation date, source. | Barrier distance is blocked when underlying mapping or observation level is missing. |
| Structured products | Physical delivery shares | `delivered_shares = nominal / strike_price` adjusted for contract terms | Delivered asset, quantity, residual cash, cost basis policy, note closure. | Direct asset position is created only after legal delivery, not before final fixing. |
| Structured notes | Coupon schedule projection | `projected_coupon = nominal x coupon_rate x accrual_fraction` when unconditional; conditional coupons require observation rule. | Coupon schedule, projected/confirmed state, payment date, source terms. | Conditional coupons are not reported as confirmed income before condition is met. |
| Structured notes | Issuer concentration | `issuer_exposure = sum(note_market_values_by_issuer)` | Issuer exposure, product family exposure, maturity/call ladder. | Issuer exposure includes notes even when underlying exposure belongs to another asset class. |
| Structured notes | Credit event write-down | `post_event_value = nominal x recovery_or_write_down_factor` | Credit event status, write-down, recovery assumption, source notice. | Credit event processing requires issuer/source notice and should preserve prior valuation state. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Allocation correction delta | `quantity_delta = corrected_allocated_quantity - original_allocated_quantity` | Original allocation, corrected allocation, quantity delta, cash delta, lot delta, approval evidence. | Position, cash, tax lots, performance and reports update with lineage to the correction. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Late fee/tax posting | `total_cash_cost = gross_consideration + commission + exchange_fee + stamp_duty + other_taxes` | Gross consideration, fees, taxes, total cash cost, cost-basis policy, source file. | Late fee/tax files create linked adjustments rather than unexplained cash breaks. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Trade correction cash delta | `cash_delta = original_consideration - corrected_consideration` | Original trade, corrected trade, cash delta, reason code, impacted reports. | Corrected trade preserves original booking and triggers impact assessment. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Reconciliation break | `break_amount = internal_balance - external_source_balance` | Internal balance, source balance, break components, tolerance, closure state. | Break closes only after component-level evidence or approved exception is recorded. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Settlement netting | `net_settlement_cash = sum(sell_proceeds) - sum(buy_consideration) - fees_and_taxes` | Gross trades, value date, broker, custodian, netting rule, net cash movement. | Gross trades remain visible while external cash can reconcile to a net movement. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Market claim | `claim_amount = affected_quantity x entitlement_per_unit` | Corporate-action event, ex-date, record date, trade, affected quantity, entitlement rate, tax treatment. | Entitlement mismatch creates receivable/payable workflow rather than manual income overwrite. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Omnibus allocation check | `allocation_break = custodian_omnibus_quantity - sum(internal_sub_account_quantity)` | Omnibus position, internal sub-account allocations, pending orders, NAV/price date, rounding policy. | External omnibus custody reconciles to internal client-level holdings with privacy controls. |
| Trade lifecycle, settlement, custody and operations | Custody transfer balance | `net_reporting_perimeter_change = transfer_in_quantity - transfer_out_quantity` | Source account, target account, quantity, transfer type, lot basis, reporting perimeter. | Internal custody transfer does not create sale proceeds, realized P&L or external cashflow. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Treaty withholding | `withholding = gross_income x configured_rate` using documentation and jurisdiction rule | Gross income, source country, documentation status, rate, withholding, net income. | Treaty rate applies only when documentation is valid for the event date. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Withholding reclaim receivable | `expected_reclaim = tax_withheld - expected_treaty_tax - reclaim_fees` | Tax withheld, expected treaty tax, reclaim fee, expected reclaim, status. | Expected reclaim is not available cash until refund receipt is confirmed. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Amended report delta | `delta = corrected_reported_amount - original_reported_amount` | Original amount, corrected amount, delta, reason code, amended output version. | Original report output is preserved and corrected output carries approval lineage. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Report-output reconciliation | `reconciliation_break = report_total - sum(source_event_amounts)` | Report total, source total, break amount, source count, exception status. | Report cannot be marked final while material source/report totals are unreconciled. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Transferred tax-lot reconciliation | `lot_quantity_break = transferred_position_quantity - sum(received_lot_quantities)` | Transferred quantity, received lot quantities, basis, acquisition dates, custodian source. | Realized-gain reporting remains partial or blocked until transferred lots reconcile. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Multi-currency realized gain | `reporting_ccy_result = sale_value_local x sale_fx - cost_value_local x acquisition_fx` | Local proceeds, local cost, acquisition FX, sale FX, local gain, reporting-currency result. | Local economic result and reporting-currency result are labelled separately. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Withholding pool break | `pool_break = pool_file_total - sum(client_allocated_withholding)` | Pool total, client allocations, break amount, threshold, approval state. | Final output is blocked or labelled when material pool breaks remain unresolved. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Delivery entitlement | `delivery_allowed = recipient_role in allowed_roles_for_report_scope` with effective-dated authority | Recipient role, report scope, entity, effective date, delivery version. | Generated reports are not delivered to unauthorized recipients or scopes. |
| Tax, regulatory reporting and cross-border reporting | Late tax-breakdown delta | `classification_delta = final_classification_amount - estimated_classification_amount` | Estimated breakdown, final breakdown, received date, impacted reports, correction decision. | Estimated and final classifications are both preserved with correction lineage. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Treasury bill discount yield | `discount_yield = (face_value - price) / face_value x 360 / days_to_maturity` | Face value, discount price, maturity date, yield convention, maturity proceeds. | Yield label must state discount-yield convention and should not be confused with bond-equivalent yield. |
| Cash, deposits, money market and FX | Repo collateral cash | `repo_cash = collateral_market_value x (1 - haircut)` before fees and margin calls | Collateral value, pledged amount, haircut, repo cash, maturity, counterparty. | Pledged securities are not treated as fully available liquidity while repo is open. |
| Bonds | Floating-rate note coupon reset | `coupon_rate = reference_fixing + spread`, adjusted for cap/floor; `coupon = nominal x coupon_rate x day_count_fraction` | Reference rate, spread, cap/floor, reset date, current coupon, next pay date. | Coupon accrual changes only after the source-backed reset fixing and terms are applied. |
| Funds | Swing-priced redemption | `swung_nav = published_nav x (1 - swing_factor)` for a redemption swing | Published NAV, swing factor, swung NAV, redeemable units, settlement cash. | Redemption proceeds use the confirmed swung NAV where the fund applies swing pricing. |
| Funds | Side-pocket split | `side_pocket_units = original_units x side_pocket_percentage`; `redeemable_units = original_units - side_pocket_units` | Redeemable units, restricted units, side-pocket source, liquidity state. | Side-pocket units remain restricted and cannot be used as immediate rebalance funding. |
| Equities | Tender offer mixed consideration | `accepted_shares = elected_shares x proration`; `cash = accepted_shares x cash_per_share`; `new_shares = accepted_shares x exchange_ratio` | Election status, accepted shares, cash consideration, share consideration, residual position. | Position changes only for accepted shares after proration and source confirmation. |
| Insurance and annuities | Participating policy declared bonus | `updated_cash_value = prior_cash_value + declared_bonus`; projected bonus remains separate | Cash value, declared bonus, projected bonus, quote date, guarantee basis. | Declared and projected bonuses are labelled separately and projected value is not current market value. |
| Insurance and annuities | Assigned policy accessible value | `accessible_value = cash_value - policy_loan - assignment_restriction_amount - charges` | Cash value, loan balance, assignment state, release status, accessible value. | Assigned policy value is not treated as freely available collateral until release evidence is sourced. |
| Structured products | Structured deposit conditional coupon | `coupon = principal x annual_coupon_rate x tenor_fraction` if condition met, otherwise zero unless terms state otherwise | Principal, payoff condition, observation result, confirmed coupon, maturity cash. | Principal protection is labelled as maturity protection and not current break value. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Investment committee threshold | `post_trade_weight = (existing_exposure + proposed_exposure) / consolidated_reportable_assets` | Existing exposure, proposed exposure, consolidated asset base, threshold, approval status. | Committee approval is required before order or commitment when post-trade weight breaches source-backed threshold. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | VCC sub-fund reportable exposure | `reportable_exposure = held_sub_fund_value`; non-held sub-funds are excluded unless authorized | Umbrella, sub-fund id, held value, excluded value, reporting perimeter, privacy state. | Reports do not overstate exposure by using umbrella-level assets when only one sub-fund is held. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Beneficiary-class visibility | `visible_sections = sections_allowed_by_role_and_governing_document` | Beneficiary class, permitted sections, masked sections, effective date, source document. | Named beneficiary status alone does not grant holdings, capital or trustee-note visibility. |
| Trusts, estate, family office and wealth structuring | Effective-dated authority change | `active_authority = authority where effective_from <= action_date < effective_to` | Role, authority holder, effective dates, document evidence, pending approvals. | Old authority cannot approve new actions after effective revocation or replacement date. |

## Reporting Label Rules

| Output Type | Required Label |
|---|---|
| Calculated value | Formula basis, source date, currency, rounding, and supportability state. |
| Projected value | Projection assumptions, source, date, and non-guaranteed status. |
| Guaranteed value | Guarantee provider, conditions, date, and excluded assumptions. |
| Estimated value | Estimate basis, stale/missing source explanation, and review status. |
| Income | Gross/net basis, tax/fee treatment, confirmation state, and payment date. |
| Exposure | Legal holding versus analytical exposure versus look-through exposure. |
| Liquidity | Legal liquidity, operational liquidity, settlement timing, restrictions, and pledge state. |
