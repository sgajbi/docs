# 09 - Source Notes and Further Reading

## 1. Important note

This knowledge pack is for financial-product, wealth-platform and reporting-domain learning. It is not tax advice and should not be used as a substitute for qualified legal, tax, compliance or regulatory advice.

Use official sources for final interpretation and always verify current reporting deadlines, schemas, thresholds, forms and local tax rules.

## 3. How to use these sources in platform work

Use these sources to inform:

- data fields required for client/entity/account documentation;
- CRS/FATCA annual reporting workflows;
- withholding and income-classification concepts;
- client tax voucher design;
- reportability status logic;
- audit and evidence retention;
- exception handling for missing or inconsistent documentation.

Do not copy legal text into code. Convert applicable rules into versioned, reviewed, testable business-rule configuration approved by tax/compliance stakeholders.
