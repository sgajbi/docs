    # Source Notes and Further Reading

    **Purpose:** professional knowledge-base material for wealth management, private banking, advisory, DPM/mandates, portfolio analytics, reporting, implementation, QA, operations and office knowledge sharing.  
    **Scope:** educational and platform-design reference only. It is not legal, tax, fiduciary or estate-planning advice. Actual client structures must be validated by qualified legal, tax, trust, compliance and jurisdiction specialists.

    ## 1. Important note

This pack is an educational and platform-design reference. Trusts, estate planning, family office structuring and tax treatment are jurisdiction-specific and can change. Always validate with qualified legal, tax, trust, fiduciary and compliance professionals before using any structure with a real client.

## 4. Implementation checklist

A platform is not ready for serious HNW/UHNW structuring unless it can answer:

- who is the legal owner?
- who is the beneficial owner?
- who can instruct?
- who can approve?
- who can see reporting?
- what document gives that authority?
- what is effective today versus historically?
- what restrictions apply to investment, leverage and distribution?
- what happens on death, incapacity, trustee change or beneficiary change?
- how does consolidation avoid double-counting?
