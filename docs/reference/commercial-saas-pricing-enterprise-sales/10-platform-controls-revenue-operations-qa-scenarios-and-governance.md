# 10 - Platform Controls, Revenue Operations, QA Scenarios and Governance

## 1. Revenue operations

Revenue operations connects:

- sales
- pricing
- quoting
- contracting
- billing
- customer success
- renewals
- expansion
- finance
- product usage
- support and SLA reporting

## 2. Governance forums

Useful forums:

| Forum | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Pricing committee | Approve pricing and discounts |
| Deal desk | Review non-standard deals |
| Product council | Align roadmap and packages |
| Customer success review | Adoption and value realization |
| Renewal review | Renewal risk and expansion |
| Security/compliance review | Enterprise evidence readiness |
| AI governance review | AI product risk and controls |
| Executive pipeline review | Strategic deals |

## 3. Control points

| Control | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Approved price book | Prevent ad hoc pricing |
| Discount approval | Protect margin |
| Contract review | Avoid risky clauses |
| SLA approval | Ensure commitments are deliverable |
| Usage metering validation | Prevent billing errors |
| Entitlement reconciliation | Match contract to product access |
| Renewal calendar | Avoid missed renewals |
| Customer health score | Identify churn risk |
| Support SLA reporting | Evidence service quality |
| AI usage control | Manage risk and cost |

## 4. QA scenarios

### Scenario 1 - Enterprise quote with discount

Expected:

- discount requires approval
- quote validity date set
- implementation fee separate
- renewal baseline clear
- margin impact calculated

### Scenario 2 - Usage overage

Expected:

- usage events captured
- non-prod excluded if contract says so
- overage calculated
- customer notified
- invoice line generated

### Scenario 3 - Contract expansion

Expected:

- amendment or new order form
- entitlement updated
- billing adjusted
- customer success notified
- support tier checked

### Scenario 4 - SLA breach

Expected:

- incident recorded
- SLA measured
- service credit calculated if applicable
- root cause captured
- customer communication tracked

### Scenario 5 - AI usage cost spike

Expected:

- quota alert triggered
- workflow/source identified
- customer/internal owner notified
- cost-control action taken
- billing impact calculated

### Scenario 6 - Customer renewal

Expected:

- renewal reminder created
- usage/adoption report generated
- open issues reviewed
- value realization summarized
- expansion opportunities identified

|---|---|
| ARR | recurring revenue scale |

| Gross margin | sustainability |
| NRR | expansion quality |
| GRR | retention quality |

## 8. Key takeaway

Commercial operations must be controlled, measurable and connected to product usage.
