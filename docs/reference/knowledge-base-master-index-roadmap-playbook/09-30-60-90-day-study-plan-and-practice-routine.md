# 09 - 30-60-90 Day Study Plan and Practice Routine

## 1. Goal

The goal is to become stronger across:

- product knowledge
- portfolio analytics
- advisory and mandates
- reporting
- platform architecture
- data quality and controls
- AI in wealth
- senior practitioner communication
- wealth-platform suite product strategy

## 2. First 30 days - Product and platform foundation

### Week 1

Study:

- bonds
- funds
- equities
- cash/FX

Practice:

- explain each product in 2 minutes
- create one lifecycle diagram each
- identify transaction types
- identify valuation source

### Week 2

Study:

- structured notes
- derivatives
- structured products
- private markets

Practice:

- explain payoff behaviour
- identify embedded risks
- model position vs exposure
- define reporting disclosures

### Week 3

Study:

- investment accounting
- market data
- trade lifecycle
- tax/regulatory reporting

Practice:

- map trade to ledger
- map price/FX to valuation
- map income to reporting
- identify data-quality controls

### Week 4

Study:

- performance/risk/benchmarks
- portfolio reporting
- data governance

Practice:

- explain TWR/MWR
- explain contribution/attribution
- explain stale price impact
- create one portfolio review structure

## 3. Days 31-60 - Advisory, DPM and enterprise architecture

### Week 5

- product governance
- suitability

- rebalancing

Practice:

- explain advisor proposal workflow

### Week 6

### Week 7

Practice:

### Week 8

Study:

- security

Practice:

- answer procurement readiness questions
- define production readiness checklist
- define security controls for AI/reporting platform

## 5. Weekly practice template

Each week produce:

```text
1. One page summary
2. One worked example
3. One platform design diagram
4. One practitioner answer
5. One QA scenario
6. One wealth-platform applicationlication idea
```

## 6. Daily 45-minute routine

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 10 min | read one section |
| 10 min | summarize from memory |
| 10 min | connect to platform/data model |
| 10 min | create practitioner answer |
| 5 min | note gaps or improvements |

## 7. Monthly checkpoint

Ask:

- Can I explain this to business?
- Can I explain this to developers?
- Can I explain this to QA?
- Can I design a data model?
- Can I identify controls?
- Can I answer practitioner questions?
- Can I connect it to wealth-platform suite?

## 8. Key takeaway

The goal is not to read everything once. The goal is to repeatedly convert knowledge into explanation, design and judgment.
