# Vendor Management, Third-Party Risk, Outsourcing, Procurement and Bank-Buyable Software Controls

## Purpose

This reference pack explains how financial institutions evaluate, approve, contract, onboard, monitor and exit third-party vendors, outsourcing providers, SaaS platforms, cloud providers, fintech partners, data vendors and software suppliers.

Use it when a wealth-platform initiative depends on external software, data, AI, infrastructure, consulting, support, custody, reporting, market-data, cloud, cyber, or operations services.

## Audience

- Wealth-platform architects and engineering leads
- Product owners and business analysts
- Vendor-management, procurement, legal and risk stakeholders
- Security, privacy, resilience and audit teams
- Data, AI, analytics and reporting platform teams
- Startup or product teams preparing bank-buyable software

## Core Principle

A bank does not buy only software. It buys a controlled operating relationship.

Product capability must be supported by evidence for security, privacy, resilience, auditability, operational support, financial stability, contractual protection, safe exit and regulatory accountability.
