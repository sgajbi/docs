# 05 - Idea Generation, Next Best Action and Investment Intelligence

## 1. What "idea generation" means in wealth

Investment idea generation is not simply recommending the highest-performing product. In wealth management, an idea must be:

- relevant to the client objective
- allowed for the client segment and booking centre
- suitable for the client risk profile
- aligned with mandate or advisory service model
- supported by product governance and approved product universe
- feasible given liquidity, cash, collateral and tax/reporting considerations
- explainable in terms of benefits, risks and alternatives

## 2. Idea types

| Idea type | Example |
|---|---|
| Portfolio rebalance | Reduce overweight US equities; increase short-duration bonds. |
| Product idea | Consider an approved investment-grade bond maturing in 3 years. |
| Income idea | Add dividend equity fund or bond ladder for income objective. |
| Risk reduction | Reduce issuer concentration or FX exposure. |
| Liquidity idea | Hold more cash for capital call or upcoming liability. |
| Tax/reporting idea | Avoid event that creates unwanted reporting/tax complexity. |
| Mandate correction | Resolve breach or drift. |
| Thematic idea | AI infrastructure, healthcare, energy transition, income resilience. |
| Lifecycle idea | Reinvest maturity proceeds or coupon cash. |

## 4. Signals for idea generation

| Signal | Example |
|---|---|
| Cash signal | High idle cash beyond target cash buffer. |
| Maturity signal | Bond/note maturing in next 30 days. |
| Concentration signal | Single issuer exceeds threshold. |
| Drift signal | Asset class outside target allocation band. |
| Risk signal | Drawdown or volatility above tolerance. |
| Income signal | Income shortfall versus client objective. |
| FX signal | Unhedged currency exposure inconsistent with base currency. |
| Liquidity signal | Upcoming capital call or loan repayment. |
| Product lifecycle signal | Structured note autocall or coupon event. |
| Client event signal | Retirement, inheritance, sale of business, education funding. |

## 5. Ranking logic

A practical idea-ranking score can combine deterministic and AI-assisted dimensions.

| Dimension | Example score basis |
|---|---|
| Eligibility | Binary pass/fail from product governance. |
| Suitability | Binary or risk-adjusted score from suitability rules. |
| Mandate fit | Breach/no breach and distance to limit. |
| Portfolio fit | Diversification, risk budget, allocation gap. |
| Liquidity fit | Cash availability and holding-period constraint. |
| Client preference | Objectives, exclusions, prior behavior. |
| Evidence strength | House view confidence, research support, data freshness. |
| Actionability | Tradability, minimum size, settlement, docs. |
| Explainability | Can the rationale be explained clearly? |

Important: AI can help rank and explain, but eligibility and suitability should be deterministic rule-driven wherever possible.

## 6. Next Best Action

Next Best Action is broader than investment recommendation.

Examples:

- schedule portfolio review
- discuss concentration risk
- collect missing suitability update
- renew expiring product documentation
- review maturing bond proceeds
- ask client about liquidity need
- follow up on unsigned proposal
- investigate stale price before report publication
- explain mandate drift to portfolio manager

## 7. Idea explanation structure

A good idea explanation should include:

```text
Why this idea surfaced
Portfolio problem/opportunity it addresses
Product or action proposed
Expected benefit
Key risk
Suitability and mandate status
Liquidity/cash/collateral impact
Alternatives
Required disclosure/approval
Source evidence
```

## 8. Example - bond maturity reinvestment idea

Scenario:

- USD 500,000 investment-grade bond matures next month
- client has income objective
- risk profile is moderate
- portfolio is underweight fixed income

AI-assisted output:

```text
Idea: Review reinvestment of upcoming USD bond maturity into a ladder of short-to-medium duration investment-grade bonds or approved bond funds.

Reason: The portfolio will receive USD 500,000 cash from maturity. Without reinvestment, cash allocation will rise above target and income yield may fall. Fixed income is currently below the advisory target band.

Key risks: interest-rate risk, issuer credit risk, reinvestment risk and liquidity. Confirm client's time horizon and income requirement before proposing products.

Controls: use only approved products for the client booking centre and risk profile; check issuer concentration and minimum denomination.
```

## 9. Example - concentration reduction idea

Scenario:

- client has 18% exposure to one technology stock
- moderate risk profile
- portfolio drawdown sensitivity is high

AI-assisted output:

```text
Idea: Discuss reducing single-name concentration or hedging part of the exposure.

Reason: The portfolio has a high single-name exposure that drives a large share of equity risk. Reducing exposure may improve diversification and reduce downside sensitivity.

Alternatives: partial sale, collar strategy, diversified equity fund, phased reduction, no action with documented rationale.

Controls: check tax implications, client preference, restricted-list status, liquidity and suitability before action.
```

## 10. Guardrails

| Guardrail | Requirement |
|---|---|
| APU only | Only recommend approved products or label as research/watchlist. |
| Suitability first | Unsuitable ideas must be suppressed or escalated. |
| Mandate first | DPM mandate breaches must block trade proposal. |
| No hallucinated products | Product must exist in product master/APU. |
| Evidence required | House view, analytics or portfolio signal must support idea. |
| Human approval | Advisor/PM approves before client communication. |
| Alternatives | Material alternatives should be shown for high-impact ideas. |
| Risk wording | Downside and liquidity risks must be included. |

## 11. Feedback loop

Capture:

- advisor accepted/rejected idea
- reason for rejection
- client accepted/rejected proposal
- trade executed or not
- subsequent outcome
- complaints or escalation
- suitability override reason
- report narrative corrections

Do not blindly optimize for sales conversion. Optimize for suitable, explainable and client-aligned outcomes.
