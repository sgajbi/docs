# 11 - Source Notes and Further Reading

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) and Generative AI Profile.
- ISO/IEC 42001:2023 - AI management system standard.
- Singapore Model AI Governance Framework / AI Verify Foundation materials.
- MAS FEAT principles for responsible use of AI and data analytics in financial services.
- EU AI Act official text and high-risk AI obligations where applicable.

## 2. LLM and GenAI security

- OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications 2025.
- OWASP GenAI Security Project resources.
- NIST cybersecurity and privacy risk-management references.
- Enterprise prompt-injection, tool-use and data-leakage security literature.

## 3. AI testing and assurance

- AI Verify Testing Framework.
- Project Moonshot LLM evaluation toolkit.
