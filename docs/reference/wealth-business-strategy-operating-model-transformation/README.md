# Wealth Business Strategy, Operating Model, Target Operating Model and Transformation Roadmap

## Purpose

This reference pack connects product, platform, analytics, reporting, AI, governance, security, delivery and commercial knowledge into an executive-level transformation view for wealth management.

Use it for strategy, capability mapping, target operating model design, roadmap sequencing, modernization, migration, country rollout, governance, value realization and executive communication.

- Wealth technology leaders
- Platform and enterprise architects
- Product owners and business analysts
- Transformation and delivery leads
- Portfolio analytics and reporting leaders
- Advisory, DPM, risk, compliance and operations stakeholders
- Product teams building enterprise wealth software
