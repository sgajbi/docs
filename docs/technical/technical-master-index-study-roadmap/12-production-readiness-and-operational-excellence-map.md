## Go-Live Review Questions

- Is service owner clear?
- Are dashboards ready?
- Are alerts actionable?
- Are runbooks current?
- Are critical tests passing?
- Is rollback defined?
- Are migrations tested?
- Are support teams trained?
- Are known issues documented?
- Are security risks accepted or resolved?
- Are business stakeholders ready?
- Is evidence retained?

## Summary

Production readiness is a cross-guide discipline.

It requires runtime, security, observability, support, testing, documentation, and leadership alignment.
