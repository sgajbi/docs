# Practitioner And Leadership Readiness Roadmap

## Purpose

This file explains how to use the knowledge-base series for senior technical leadership narratives, practitioner discussions, and executive-level conversations.

The goal is to speak as a domain-aware engineering leader who understands business, architecture, delivery, and production.

---

## Leadership Readiness Themes

Prepare around:

- technical strategy
- architecture governance
- service ownership
- API/data governance
- cloud/runtime modernization
- security and risk
- production support
- migration/replatforming
- data lineage and reconciliation
- wealth domain expertise
- AI adoption
- engineering culture
- stakeholder communication

---

## Example Leadership Framing

Instead of:

```text
We migrated the application.
```

Say:

```text
We treated the migration as continuity engineering. We mapped data, contracts, reports, entitlements, and support processes; ran reconciliation and parallel comparison; used cutover governance; and retained evidence for sign-off and hypercare.
```
